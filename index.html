Established in 1948, Bharucha & Co. stands among the oldest law firms in Pakistan. Over more than seven decades we have continually diversified our services to meet evolving client needs.

Our practice spans intellectual property, company law, real estate and property, inheritance and succession, not-for-profit entities, franchising and litigation. Our associates share skills and resources to obtain optimal results for every client.

We have been ranked by various legal referral guides as one of the best intellectual property law firms in Pakistan — serving local and international clients across industries.
